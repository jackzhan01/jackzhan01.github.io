Hi! I am **Wangjia (Jack) Zhan**, currently a **M.S. student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC)**. My current research centers on **agentic systems** and **multi-agent systems**, as well as **LLM-driven code optimization**. I am also broadly interested in **large-scale machine learning**, including **scaling laws** and **efficient training/fine-tuning algorithms**, with earlier work on **deep learning-based recommendation systems (DLRS)**.

I am a **Research Assistant supervised by Prof. Minjia Zhang (UIUC CS)**, working on **agentic systems** and **efficient large-scale machine learning**.
